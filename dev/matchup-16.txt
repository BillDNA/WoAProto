Skill-vs-luck report: 16 battles per map per pairing, 12 maps.
Each pairing also swaps sides so colour bias cancels out.

............  normal vs easy     stronger AI wins 71% of 192
............  hard vs normal     stronger AI wins 57% of 192
............  hard vs easy       stronger AI wins 80% of 192
............  normal vs normal   stronger AI wins 46% of 192

How to read it: a clearly better player winning only ~50-55% means the
card draw decides most battles (luck-heavy). 55-65% = luck and skill both
matter. 65%+ = skill dominates. The normal-vs-normal line is the ~50% sanity check.
