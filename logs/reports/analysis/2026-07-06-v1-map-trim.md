# V1 map trim: 17 → 12 (July 2026)

Bill's Round-5 ask: *"trim down to 12 maps"*, method left to judgment using the
reports we already had. Decision basis: the 0.3 accumulated data (n=60/map,
hard-vs-hard) cross-checked against a fresh 1.0 run (n=24/map, hard-vs-hard,
post-AI-overhaul + same-type-swap ban). Balance score = |Red−50| + |1st−50| +
0.6·0kill% + 0.3·tie% + 0.4·drag − 3·swings (lower = better).

Note the original 0.3-only cut list had Twin Gates + The Ford instead of The
Bulge + Twin Woods; both improved markedly under the 1.0 rules/AI while the two
classics degraded, so the final cut follows the combined evidence (1.0 is the
era going forward; its n=24 alone is too noisy to rule, hence the sum).

## Coverage after the cut (12 survivors)

- **Shapes**: compact ×3 (Causeway, Ford, Cockpit) · classic ×3 (Killing
  Ground, Frontier, Riverbend) · hourglass ×2 (Twin Gates, Narrows) ·
  spear (Long March) · ridge (Saber Ridge) · custom ×2 (Void, Marshes).
- **Terrain niches**: heavy forest F4 → Long March, Narrows (covers Black
  Forest's niche) · heavy mountain M4 → Saber Ridge (covers Open Mountain
  Pass's) · rivers → Causeway, Ford, Riverbend (R4) + Marshes (R2).
- All three cuts beyond the two-era losers were F2M2R0 standards whose niche
  is covered twice over. Nothing collapses.

**Watchlist for the next trim/expansion round**: Causeway (tie-decided %),
Killing Ground + The Void (1.0 wobble — recheck at n≥60).
